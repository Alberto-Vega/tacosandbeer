ABOUT

Grandma's recipes and a desire to share our experience.

When we arrived to Seattle we realized that we couldn't find a Mexican restaurant that we fully like and we really missed the good food we had while growing up back in Baja California.
So we took matters in our own hands and gathered our family recipes and put together a menu that allowed us to eat just like we did at Grandma's house when we were kids. Tacos and Beer was born since we wanted to share our food with everyone.
